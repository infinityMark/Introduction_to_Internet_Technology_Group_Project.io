The primary aim of designing this website is:

  • to centralize a wide range of essential academic and administrative functions into a single, unified digital platform.
  • it seeks to greatly enhance convenience and efficiency for students, teachers, and school staff alike.

    • For students, the platform serves as a one-stop portal to:

  • access class schedules
  • submit assignments
  • receive announcements
  • communicate with instructors and
  • monitor academic progress.

    • For teachers, it streamlines key tasks such as:

  • managing class materials
  • grading and
  • posting updates or resources.

    • Administrative staff can benefit from:

  • improved coordination,
  • simplified document management, and
  • enhanced communication channels across departments.

    • Ultimately, this system is designed to reduce fragmentation across various tools and platforms currently in use, replacing them with an integrated solution that supports the digital transformation of the school environment and promotes a more seamless user experience for all stakeholders.
